About Us

VISION: We aim to be the leading Sports Infrastructure and Education company in India, empowering India to become a sporting powerhouse.
MISSION: We want to provide young footballers across the country with the best sporting facilities and education to hone their game, thus enabling and encouraging them to embrace a sporting culture. Score was born out of the passion of our founding Directors. This passion is at the cornerstone of everything we do. Our directors Pranav and Vidur have been actively involved in the sporting scene in India and abroad. Score is their first step in helping and developing sports in India from the grassroots level. Score is constantly evolving and with the technical backing of MarwahSphere, aims to be the premier player in the Sports Infrastructure and Education sector in India .....more
